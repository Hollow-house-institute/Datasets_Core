# Dataset License Addendum — OPS Series

This addendum applies specifically to all datasets under the OPS namespace
within the Datasets_Core repository.

## Scope

Covered materials include:
- OPS datasets
- OPS-derived metadata
- OPS indexes, schemas, and provenance files

This addendum does not replace or supersede the root LICENSE.md.
It constrains dataset use under that license.

## Restrictions

Unless explicitly authorized in writing:

- No reuse for model training (including fine-tuning, embeddings, or evaluation)
- No commercial use
- No redistribution or mirroring
- No derivative datasets
- No incorporation into automated decision systems

## Permitted Use

- Private review
- Governance, audit, and standards reference
- Non-derivative academic citation

Unauthorized use constitutes a license violation.
